Whiskey sour

This quick and easy drink recipe for the gentelmans after a long day.

Ingredients

  • 45 ml Bourbon Whiskey
  • 25 ml Fresh Lemon Juice
  • 20 ml Sugar Syrup
  • Few Drops of Egg White (Optional)

Steps

  1. Pour all ingredients into cocktail shaker filled with ice. Shake well.
  2. Strain into cobbler glass. If served “On the rocks”, strain ingredients into old fashioned glass filled with ice.
  3. If egg white is used shake little harder to release and incorporate the foam from the egg white.
  4. Garnish with half orange slice and maraschino cherry, optionally use orange zest.
